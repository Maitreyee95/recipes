Ingredients:
 - 1/2 cup molten butter
 - 2/3 cup cocoa
 - 3 cups powdered sugar
 - 1/3 cup milk
 - 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
 - Crushed nuts

Directions:
  1. In a cup, add butter,cocoa and powdered sugar.
  2. Add milk while stirring to avoid lump.
  3. Preheat oven at 180 deg.
  4. Stir in vanilla.  Pour the mixture into cup.
  5. Sprinkle nuts on top.
  6. Bake for 5 mins.
